Credentialing

We can manage your credentialing with no hassel so you can focus on your patients and administrative tasks efficiently. We can manage to help you in credentialing and re-credentialing process; As a new Client checking your EDI Setup with Insurance, so that your claims went through smoothly by doing due diligence from EDI to 24hours test claim submission Setting up an ERA, EFT with your desired EHR Updating CAQH portal Completing documentation and submitting to regular and private payer’s state wise. Credentialing, Re-Credentialing & Auditing with regular and private payers Filling Medicare Enrollment Forms Scenario-based Supporting Documentation Completion of CMS 588 (EFT), CMS460 (New Provider Agreement), CMS855i (Individual Provider), CMS855R (Reassignment) and CMS855B (Group Provider), PEHIRS Managing overall credentialing application cycle from submission to follow-up till successfully credentialed. Managing PECOS portal database and RFI’s (Request for Information)
